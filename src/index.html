About

Hey hacker! Westview Hacks is a hackathon where you will code fun & crazy projects with others or individually. Think of it as a 10-hour coding marathon (with time for food and breaks!) Compete until the end to win cool prizes! We encourage and welcome any middle and high student to participate, regardless of programming experience.
